### 1. From Memorization → Visualization

1. **“Chemistry isn’t hard—you’ve just never seen it in motion.”**  
   Why it worked: Reframes difficulty as lack of visualization.

2. **“What if reactions didn’t just happen on paper—but in front of you?”**  
   Why it worked: Visual + curiosity-driven.

3. **“Stop memorizing equations. Start watching molecules interact.”**

4. **“The problem with chemistry isn’t complexity—it’s invisibility.”**  
   Why it worked: Identifies core learning barrier.
